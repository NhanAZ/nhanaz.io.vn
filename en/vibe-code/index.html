Vibe-code

Projects built through quick experiments with AI, code, instinct, and manual fixes until they become something useful.

How I tell it

Each project gets a short story

I do not want this section to exist just to say, ‘AI was used here’. The better story is where a project began, what AI sped up, what I had to fix, where it failed, and what was still worth keeping at the end.

Vibe-code here means moving quickly without being careless. It may start with a prompt, an empty repository, or a slightly silly idea, but the finished post should still explain why the project exists.
